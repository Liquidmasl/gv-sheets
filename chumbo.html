Rage & toughness

Rage 3 / long rest · 1 gets back on short rest
+2 damage on STR attacks · half damage from slashing/piercing/bludgeoning · advantage on STR checks & saves. Stays up if you attack or take damage each round (max 10 min). No heavy armor. Entering rage = roll on your Wild Magic table below!
Reckless Attack
Declare on your first attack: advantage on all your STR attacks this turn — but attacks against you also have advantage until your next turn.
Stone's Endurance 1 / short rest
Reaction when damaged: reduce it by 1d12+3.
Danger Sense
Advantage on DEX saves (unless Incapacitated). Fireballs fear you.
Primal Knowledge
While raging: Acrobatics, Intimidation, Perception, Stealth, Survival checks can use STR (+7). Yes — angry sneaking.
Magic Awareness 3 / long rest
Action: sense every spell and magic item within 60 ft (and its school) until your next turn ends. The wild magic in you sniffs out other magic.
Wild Magic surge (d8, roll when you Rage) — save DC 14
